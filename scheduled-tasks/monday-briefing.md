# Monday Morning AI Briefing

Every Monday at 8 AM: compile AI news and message you a briefing.

## Schedule
Cron: `0 8 * * 1`

## Sources
- Hacker News (top AI posts from last 7 days)
- TechCrunch AI section
- Anthropic blog
- GitHub trending (AI repos)

## Trigger word
`@Andy every Monday at 8am, compile news on AI developments from Hacker News and TechCrunch and message me a briefing`

## Output
Sent to your main WhatsApp/Telegram channel as a formatted digest.
